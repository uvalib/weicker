About the Collection

The Lowell P. Weicker, Jr., Papers Collection is housed at the Albert and Shirley Small Special Collections Library at the University of Virginia in Charlottesville. It was donated to the University in 2008, and the majority of the collection has been processed and is available for research. Please contact the Special Collections Library for access to the materials.
