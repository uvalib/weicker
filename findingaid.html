Weicker Finding Aids

The Lowell P. Weicker, Jr., Papers Collection consists of over 2,000 boxes of material covering Weicker’s involvement in the Watergate hearings, legislation regarding mental disabilities, the Americans with Disabilities Act, public health and the AIDS epidemic, transportation, ocean research and protection, and other major 20th century political issues. Please contact the Special Collections Library for access to the materials.
