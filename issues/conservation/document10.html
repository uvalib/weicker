Guest Editorial on Conservation

Conservation 88 Guest Editorial Article
Draft with markup

Senator Weicker's article, eventually titled "A Voyage of Discovery," discussed the need for ocean research and protection. Weicker addressed issues such as mass dolphin deaths, coral bleaching, pollution, environmental deterioration, and the need to develop technology to "explore and better understand the ocean depths and a government agency." With budget cuts looming, Weicker warned against cutting funding to NOAA and the Hydro-lab off St. Croix, and emphasized "sustainable development of our marine resources." Finally, Weicker highlighted promising legislative initiatives like the National Marine Policy Commission and the creation of the American Oceans Campaign. This article was published in the National Wildlife Federation's Conservation newsletter, Vol. 6, No. 6 May 1998. Below is Weicker's working draft with markup.
