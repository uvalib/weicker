S. 771: Amtrak and the Northeast Corridor
March 18, 1987

S. 771, cosponsored by Senator Lowell Weicker, grants Congress the right to make rail improvements in the Northeast corridor. With the enactment of this bill, Amtrak continued its "operational authority in the Northeast Corridor and â€¦ provide[s] funding for high speed capital improvements." Senator Weicker's support of these improvements ensured Connecticut residents (and other Northeasterners) had reduced travel time between Washington, D.C. and Boston, Massachusetts, and creates a commission to oversee the implementation of improvements and maintenance of the rail system. Weicker, a longtime supporter of Amtrak, remained loyal to public transportation up through the end of his Senatorial tenure.
