S. 1220 Speech

June 1988

Copy of typed speech on S. 1220 a Comprehensive Federal Aids Program legislative initiative. In this speech, Senator Weicker encouraged the passage of the bill to provide funding to AIDS prevention and research. Weicker criticized the federal government's current response to the AIDS epidemic and request funding for the NIH in FY (fiscal year) '88.

A key part of the bill and Weicker's speech emphasized AIDS prevention education to children. Senator Weicker outlines six ways states may spend federal funds. Weicker encouraged the government to "educate and inform [young people], even...in the midst of all other complex aspects of sexual relations in America." The Senator and his supporters saw education and prevention as life and death issues and urged the passage of comprehensive AIDS legislation.
